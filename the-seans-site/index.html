The product idea

01

Start with the timetable.

The schedule is the most reliable source of truth in college. It tells us when a student moves, where they go, and which communities they belong to.

02

Reveal the people around it.

ClassMate makes classmates visible in context, so social features begin with a real shared class instead of a cold search bar.

03

Keep conversations attached.

Boards, course links, marketplace posts, and DMs become more useful when they know which school, department, or class they came from.

04

Scale only when the campus is ready.

We start at UCI, then expand school by school with dependable course data, moderation, and a product students actually trust.

Company motto

Make college less scattered.

College is not one app. It is a schedule, a course search, a room number, a Discord link, a board post, a group message, a sports event, and a reminder you almost forgot. the seans exists to make those pieces feel like they belong together.

Why we started

The timetable is the most honest map of student life.

Every quarter, students rebuild the same context from scratch. They add classes, hunt for group chats, ask who else is enrolled, look for campus events, and spread information across tools that do not know what school or class they belong to.

We started with the schedule because it is the one place where academic life and social life naturally meet.

First product

ClassMate.

The campus app that starts with your timetable, then organizes everything around it.

Built from campus life

Not another feed. A calmer operating layer.

Students already have enough places to scroll. Our job is to make the important campus context easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to act on.

Founders

Founded by Kyumin Kwack and Sihyun Park.

the seans began as a student-built product from UC Irvine. Kyumin and Sihyun started with a practical belief: college software should understand a student's actual campus life instead of forcing them to rebuild context across five different tools every quarter.

The company is early by design. We are learning with real students, solving one campus deeply, and turning those lessons into a product that can travel.

Operating principles

Professional software. Student-native instincts.

Context beats volume.

One useful class-specific link is better than another endless feed.

Social, lightly.

Classmates, DMs, and boards should help students coordinate without turning the app into noise.

Respect existing tools.

We do not need to replace Discord, Canvas, calendars, or group chats. We make them easier to find in context.

Campus by campus.

Start at UCI, learn in public, then expand only where the data and student need are real.

Roadmap

From one campus to a trusted campus layer.

  1. Now

    UCI beta

    Timetable, ClassMates, boards, sports events, course Discord links, notifications, and grade tools.

  2. Next

    Reliable campus graph

    Better course metadata, friend overlap, department boards, event participation, moderation, and richer onboarding.

  3. Later

    More schools

    UC campuses first as coming soon, then new schools when course data is dependable enough to earn trust.
